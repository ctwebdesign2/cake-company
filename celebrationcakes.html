CELEBRATION CAKES  |  Physics and Chemistry

Sine waves and crystalline structures were the inspiration for this cake. Cake making is Chemistry after all, along with a bit of Physics needed in tiered cakes. For the Sine waves I used the texture sheets from A Cake Story. The crystalline tier is represented with fondant circles infilled with multi-coloured sprinkles. Decorated with handmade wafer paper foliage.
