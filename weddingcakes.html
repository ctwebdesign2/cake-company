WEDDING CAKE  |  “Be Mine Forever”

“Will you be mine forever …?” Love birds gazing into each other’s eyes on a tall, elegant, four-tier, wedding cake in the ballroom of Carton House Hotel.

Chocolate with Vanilla butter-cream filling, Lemon Poppy-seed with lemon butter-cream and lemon curd filling and Coconut and Lime with lime butter-cream filling.

A rich fruit cake baked by a relative of the bride was decorated with hand-cut fondant lace to match the beautiful bridal gown.

WEDDING CAKE  |  Christmas Wedding Cake

A wedding cake for a wonderful couple with whom I was never able to meet in person to discuss their dream cake. They both loved Christmas and said they would like holly leaves to be incorporated into the design.

What began as a white cake with sugar flowers evolved into a rich green cake showing off Holly leaves and Hellebore, the Christmas Rose.
